✨ Final Documentation

Artist Statement

This is a group project which we working together to create a modular system with our own designs. We had spent a lot of time figuring out ideas and which one work best in terms of timing. After all the discussions, we decided to go with making a data visualizer for emotions using acrylic designed pieces. Not only we learned a lot about class materials, but we also learned about each other as a group, who is best at doing what, managing time and tasks to make sure it is delivered on the due date. My favorite part of this project is filming footage as we have our designs printed. We gathered together and made short funny skits and film each other outside the classroom on campus, and had so much fun together. We also had boba after that!

Successes of the Project

"DVD" succeed in delivering an entertaining and meaningful message of visualizing your emotions in a specific period of time as to keep track of your overall mental health. Viewers are able to understand how to use it easily and enjoy interacting with the pieces, as well as coming up with their own unique interpretations and ideas for the designs. Technical wise, all pieces of designs are well created and nicely cut by the laser cutting machine. Material's quality is nice and satisfying to look at.

Future Improvements

Our group can improve this project by review each other's designs. I noticed that some of our designs unexpectedly broke when we tested attaching the pieces together. The reason for that was because those specific designs had too small and thin details near, or the line cut was too near the notches. Therefore, if just a bit of over forcing it would break that piece.
